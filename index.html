Minecraft is a 3D sandbox game developed by Mojang Studios, where the player interacts with the game world by placing and destroying various blocks in three different dimensions. The varied gameplay allows players to choose their own paths through the game, opening up countless possibilities.

Roblox is an open world online game where players can create and build literally anything. Players can create literally anything - from racing tracks to simple virtual chats in which they can communicate with friends. The virtual worlds of all players are open and can be freely explored.
